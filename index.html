ABOUT
This tool uses Google Gemini AI to surface active and anticipated A/E RFPs/RFQs across Washington State's 75 public port districts. Results are AI-generated intelligence — always verify deadlines and details directly on the issuing port's procurement page before submitting.

How it works:
Clicking Scan sends your selected ports and filters to an AI model, which returns known and anticipated A/E solicitations tagged as CONFIRMED, ANTICIPATED, or RECENTLY CLOSED. Results include project title, type, discipline, deadline, and links to verify.

75 Washington Port Districts:
Washington has more public port districts than any other state. All are independent government bodies that regularly issue A/E solicitations for facility improvements, assessments, and infrastructure projects.

Best Practices:
Register on MRSC Rosters for automatic notifications. Monitor WA DES/WEBS for state-contract A/E opportunities. Always confirm details directly with the issuing port before responding to any solicitation.
